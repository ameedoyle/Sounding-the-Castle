# Sounding the Castle

Sounding the Castle is a digital humanities project documenting musical objects associated with the Castle collection.

## Collections

- Antiphoners
- Chant fragments
- Instruments
- Piano rolls
- Performances

## Featured Object

[Holy Week Antiphoner]({{ "/objects/holy-week-antiphoner/" | relative_url }}) documents a Spanish choir antiphoner from the late sixteenth or early seventeenth century.
